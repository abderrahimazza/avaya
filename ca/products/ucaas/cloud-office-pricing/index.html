A Cloud Phone System That Works Where and How You Do

Take calls, make calls, text, message, and chat. Avaya Cloud Office by RingCentral offers a seamless and intuitive communications experience that fits the way you work. One click is all it takes to start a call, join a meeting, contribute to a team chat, or share files. With over 100 app integrations, you can make Google, Salesforce, Oracle, and Microsoft part of your communications. And you can finally understand exactly how your company uses your phones with dashboards and data right at your fingertips. Voice, FAX, texting, multimedia messages: One number does it all. Avaya Cloud Office is simple communications.

Pricing is based on tiers and number of users. For more information on pricing for your business, please contact an Avaya sales representative.

Avaya Cloud Office places the Customer in control of their communications and delivers a unified communications experience that’s intuitive to use and accessible from most phones, browsers, tablets, or any mobile device.

The Service is currently available in the United States (US), Australia, Austria, Belgium, Canada, France, Germany, Ireland, Italy, the Netherlands, Portugal, Spain, and the United Kingdom, though the service can be extended from these regions to other regions via the Global Office feature.

The Avaya Cloud Office application is supported on both desktop and mobile devices and includes the following features:

  • Rich Telephony features accessible on a desk phone, desktop, or mobile device.
  • Messaging – including direct and SMS.
  • Video meeting capabilities.
  • Faxing.
  • File sharing.
  • API integrations.

All prices are subject to change and exclusive of taxes, surcharges and fees. For full details on terms and conditions, please contact an Avaya sales representative.
